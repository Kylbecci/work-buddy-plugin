# Work Buddy — Personal Context

This is your personal context file. Use it to teach your Work Buddy about your specific job, routines, and workflows. The more context you add here, the more useful your assistant becomes.

Everything in this file is optional — fill in what's relevant to you and add more over time. You can also update this anytime by just telling your Work Buddy what to add or change.

This file is your **personal** context. **Individual projects are tracked separately** — each lives in its own folder under your projects workspace with its own `context.md` (status, cadence, deadlines, working files). Just tell your Work Buddy "start a project for X" to spin one up.

---

## Who You Are

Add details about your role, team, responsibilities, and what your day-to-day looks like. This helps your Work Buddy tailor suggestions and prioritization to your actual work.

---

## Weekly Routines

Add any recurring tasks tied to specific days. Your Work Buddy will remind you on the right days and factor these into your daily planning.

---

## Custom Workflows

Describe any multi-step processes your Work Buddy should know how to help with. Include script paths, file locations, and the order of operations.

---

## Key Contacts

People you work with regularly. Your Work Buddy uses this for meeting prep and email/Slack triage — knowing who matters helps it surface the right messages.

---

## Important Links and Locations

File paths, SharePoint folders, dashboards, or other resources your Work Buddy should know about.

---

## Notes

Anything else that helps your Work Buddy understand your work — upcoming deadlines, ongoing initiatives, things to keep an eye on.
